John's Bookmarkletarium

Bookmarklets live in the same place as bookmarks, but instead of going to a URL, they do something on the internet when you click them. Here's how to get them:

  1. 1. Click and drag a bookmarklet from below into your bookmarks toolbar.
    2. 2. Click on the new bookmark to use it. Many of these copy text to your clipboard invisibly, so just trust they're working, haha.
  2. 3. To read about what it does, click the question mark.
